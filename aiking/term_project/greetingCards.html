Greeting Cards

Basic Greeting Cards

I started making my own cards when I needed one for a certain occasion and just could not find the right one. Then other people who received my cards would ask me to make one for them, for one thing or another.  From there it lead to the desire to make cards that anyone to could enjoy. This slide show gives and example of how my cards turn out.   Each card made is unique. This is part of what makes them so special.  Thus becoming a keepsake.
